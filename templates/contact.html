Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the student performance prediction?
87% accuracy rate based on validation testing across multiple institutions.

Our prediction model has been rigorously tested and validated. However, it's important to remember that these are predictive analytics and should be used as guidance rather than absolute certainty. Many factors can influence actual student performance.

What data do you need to make a prediction?

We analyze various factors including past academic performance, attendance records, study habits, and demographic information. The more complete the data provided, the more accurate our predictions will be. All data is handled with strict confidentiality and FERPA compliance.

Can this system be integrated with our school's LMS?

Yes, we offer integration capabilities with most major Learning Management Systems including Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, and D2L. Our technical team can work with your IT department to set up a secure data transfer pipeline. Contact our support team to discuss specific integration requirements.

Is there a free trial available?
30-day free trial available for qualified educational institutions.

During this period, you'll have access to all core features with up to 500 student predictions. Contact our sales team to see if your institution qualifies and to get started with your trial account.
